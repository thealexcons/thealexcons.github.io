Mediochat: Web-based video appointments

Mediochat is online web-based video appointment application that allows doctors and GPs to call their patients remotely, through video calls.

The project was developed as a hobby during the summer of 2020, using the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic as inspiration to build a MedTech web app. The system was designed to be a real-world app, hoping it could help reduce the strain on the medical services who were overloaded due to the pandemic. The entire project was finished in around a month (I did not spend too much time on it per day).

The website was implemented using NodeJS (using Express for the web server) and Firebase in the backend to manage authentication and data storage. The frontend, created with vanilla JS (and Bootstrap), dealt with the video calling functionality (which included a text chat) using the browser’s native APIs (WebRTC) and web sockets.

Some of the important features are:

  • Doctor's can schedule appointments with patients
  • Email alerts for patients (scheduled appointment, cancellation, change of time)
  • A simple and intuitive dashboard for doctors
  • A straightforward experience for patients to enter the video call
